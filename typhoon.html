Rambler Channel Typhoon Shelter

by MC

Rambler Channel Typhoon Shelter is a typhoon shelter in the Rambler Channel, near Kwai Chung, Hong Kong. It was built in 1966 with a size of 16 hectares (40 acres) . In 2004 the typhoon shelter was 13 hectares (32 acres) in size. Rambler Channel Public Cargo Working Area is just inside the typhoon shelter for ships loading and unloading cargo.
